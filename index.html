1. Overview

Easy Live Zakat Calculator is a free Islamic finance tool that helps Muslims calculate their annual Zakat obligation. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data is collected, how it is used, and your rights.

2. Data We Collect

Data Stored Locally on Your Device

The app stores the following data exclusively on your device using AsyncStorage. This data is never transmitted to our servers, and we have no access to it:

DataPurpose
Selected countrySet default language & currency
Selected currencyDisplay prices in local currency
Selected languageApp UI language
Nisab type preferenceGold or silver Nisab
Weight unit preferenceGrams or Tola
Cached gold/silver pricesAvoid repeated API calls
Manual price entriesUser-entered override prices

Data the Developer Does NOT Collect

We (the developer) do not collect, store, or transmit:

  • Personal information (name, email, phone, address)
  • Financial data (wealth amounts, calculated Zakat values)
  • Device identifiers (IMEI, device ID)
  • Precise location data
  • Usage analytics or crash reports

3. Advertising (Google AdMob)

The app displays banner advertisements served by Google AdMob. AdMob is a third-party advertising service provided by Google LLC. To deliver ads, AdMob may collect or process the following data on its own behalf:

  • Advertising identifier (Android Advertising ID / AAID)
  • Approximate location derived from IP address
  • Device information (model, OS version, language)
  • Ad interaction events (impressions, clicks)

This data is used by Google to serve ads, measure ad performance, and may be used for personalised advertising depending on your device-level ad settings.

You can opt out of personalised advertising at any time:

  • Android: Settings → Google → Ads → Delete advertising ID / Opt out of Ads Personalization.

For details, see Google’s Advertising Privacy Policy.

4. Third-Party API Calls

The app makes network requests to the following free, public APIs to fetch live gold and silver prices and currency exchange rates:

6. Children's Privacy

This app is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any personal data from children. Google AdMob is configured to serve general-audience advertisements only. If you believe a child has provided data to a third-party service via this app, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

7. Data Security

All on-device data is stored locally using React Native's AsyncStorage, which is sandboxed to the app. It is not accessible by other apps and is deleted when the app is uninstalled. All network requests use HTTPS.

8. Data Retention

  • On-device data: retained until the app is uninstalled or you use the Reset All Data option in Settings.
  • Developer-side data: none — we have no servers storing user data.
  • AdMob data: retained per Google's data retention policies.

9. Your Rights

Since we (the developer) collect no personal data, there is nothing to access, correct, or delete on our end. To clear all locally stored preferences:

  1. Open the app → Settings tab
  2. Tap Reset All Data

Or simply uninstall the app. To control AdMob data, see Section 3.
